Experience 1: Hiking

Creativity & Activity

Learning Outcomes 1, 4, 6

This activity involved us hiking in a park and taking pictures of the nature around us. This was a new experience for me, as I only go hiking with my family a couple of times a year, and never in a group such as this one. It was also new for me, as I do not often take pictures of nature, so it was a fun thing for me to try out. Planning the experience was not as smooth as I would have hoped for it to be, but it was effective, we all were able to get there, and it was fairly well planned for a first CAS experience. Working as a group definitely made the experience more enjoyable, as it was pleasing to share the experience with a group of close friends. Additionally, working with a group on this made it much easier to follow through with the plan allowing everyone to have a better time than we would have had we done it alone. Overall I feel as though it was a great experience to grow, and learn more about nature, photography, and hiking as a whole. This experience is definitely one that I would consider doing a second time, and many more beyond that.
